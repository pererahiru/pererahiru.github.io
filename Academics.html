Extra Curricular Activities

We offer a diverse and varied programme of activities for all students
in the Junior and Senior School as part of
the school’s daily routine on all weekdays (except Wednesday).
Our programme is designed to encourage students to take on new challenges,
to try something new or to improve on the ones they enjoy and have strengths in.
As they do this, they interact with students
other than their classmates
thereby creating long-lasting friendships.
The aim of the programme is to develop self-confidence
and to widen students’ personal experiences.


The Junior School after school activities programme is from 1.45 to 2.45pm.
The Senior School activities begin at 3.00 p.m.
Non-sporting activities normally end at 4.00 p.m.
whereas sporting activities may end at 4.30 p.m. or 5.00 p.m.
This allows time for students to be taken to and from venues away from the school site
as well as allowing age-specific sessions to take place as necessary.
